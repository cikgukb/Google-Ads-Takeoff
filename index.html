⚠️ Google Ads Policy Guidelines

  • ❌ No excessive capitalization (e.g. "FREE", "BUY").
  • ❌ No excessive punctuation (e.g. "Buy here!!!", "Promo???").
  • ❌ No unsupported claims (e.g. "#1", "Best") without proof.
  • ❌ No phone numbers in ad text (use Call Assets instead).
  • ❌ No gimmicky spelling (e.g. "C l e a r a n c e").
